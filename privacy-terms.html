Privacy Policy

1. Information you provide

The App lets you prepare a home offer, request a home-value estimate, and contact us. Depending on what you choose to do, you may provide:

  • Contact details — your name, email address, and phone number, when you submit an offer or ask us to follow up.
  • Property and offer details — a property address and the offer terms you enter (price, financing, earnest money, dates, and any notes).
  • Home-value requests — a property address you enter to receive an automated estimate.

You do not need to create an account, and we do not ask you to sign in.

2. Information stored on your device

Offers and home-value estimates you create are saved locally on your device so you can review them later. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us unless you choose to email or submit it. You can delete it at any time inside the App, or by deleting the App.

3. How we use information

  • To prepare the draft offer or estimate you requested.
  • To respond to you and follow up about your real estate needs.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.

4. How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to provide the features you use:

  • Form delivery — when you submit an offer or request, your details are delivered to us through a form-delivery service (Web3Forms) and/or your own email app.
  • Home-value estimates — the property address you enter is sent to a third-party property-data provider (RentCast) to generate an automated estimate and nearby comparable sales.
  • Address suggestions — as you type an address, the text is sent to an address-lookup service (Photon, by Komoot, using OpenStreetMap data) to suggest matching addresses.
  • Service providers and legal — we may share information with vendors who help us operate the App, or when required by law.

These providers process the information only to deliver their service. We encourage you to review RentCast's, Web3Forms', and Komoot's own privacy practices.

5. What we do not do

The App contains no advertising, no analytics or tracking software, and no third-party trackers. We do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not build advertising profiles.

6. Data retention

Information you send us (such as an offer or a follow-up request) is kept as long as needed to assist you and to meet our legal and recordkeeping obligations, then deleted or anonymized. Information saved on your device remains until you delete it.

7. Your choices and rights

  • You can choose not to provide information — though some features need it to work.
  • You can delete saved offers and estimates in the App at any time.
  • You may ask us to access or delete information you have sent us by emailing the address below. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act or the California Consumer Privacy Act.

8. Security

Information is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

9. Children's privacy

The App is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
